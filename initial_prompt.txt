Use Pygame to instantiate a toroidal go board, that can accept moves, alternating between white and black "stones". The dimensions of the board should be able to be selected by the user. There should be two display modes, rectangular or a view of the actual torus. In the rectangular mode, one can (right click perhaps?) to translate any point to the center of the rectangle (opposite edges are considered adjacent). In the toroidal view one can rotate the board mesh on the torus in either toroidal coordinate direction. 

Also of course one can click on a point to make a move. Moves can be taken back, and games remembered (saved as an appropriate game file). One should also be able to read in a previously saved game file, to replay the game (with an option for exploring other alternate sequences from any point in the game).
